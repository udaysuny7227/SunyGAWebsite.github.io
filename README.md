<h1 id="short-intro"></h1>

<p>I am Tanusree Sharma- 3rd year PhD student in the Informatics at School of Information Sciences in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). 
<!--   at <a href="https://socialcomputing.web.illinois.edu/">SALT LAB</a>.  -->
My research interests are in <span style="color: #1e13bd">HCI</span>, <span style="color: #13bd19"> usable security and privacy</span>, <span style="color: #bd8213">Accessibility</span>, <span style="color: #bd4913">Data Governance & Ethics</span> domain to resolve socio-technical challenges by designing and developing accessible tools with a combination of qualitative and quantitative methods. The application domains I am focusing on are <span style="color: #370c6c">Blockchain technology</span>, <span style="color: #a57129">De-Fi</span>, and <span style="color: #8a13bd">AI</span> </p>
<p>
As a researcher, I leverage large scale <span style="color: #1e13bd">Machine Learning</span> to understand end users and developers at scale to design and develop automated tooling for end users and developers. I work in learning and applying fundamentally new capability of engineering method within user experience research to strengthen privacy technologies for being more actionable, understandable, and available to users.
</p>
